# TechNova Electronics - Frequently Asked Questions & Policies

## Shipping & Delivery
Q: What are your shipping options and delivery timelines?
A: We offer Standard Shipping (3-5 business days) and Express Shipping (1-2 business days). Orders are processed within 24 hours on business days.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, TechNova ships to over 50 countries. International shipping delivery windows vary between 7 to 14 business days depending on customs processing.

## Returns & Refunds
Q: What is your return policy?
A: We offer a 30-day return policy for all undamaged electronics. Items must be returned in their original packaging with all included accessories.

Q: How long does it take to receive a refund?
A: Once we receive and inspect your returned item, your refund will be processed back to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

## Technical Support & Warranty
Q: Do your products come with a warranty?
A: All TechNova hardware products include a standard 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. It does not cover accidental damage or water damage.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can reach our support team via email at support@technova-example.com or through our live chat portal active from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST.
